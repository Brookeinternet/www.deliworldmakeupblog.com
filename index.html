Mastering the Perfect Smoky Eye

The smoky eye is a timeless look that can be adapted for any occasion, from a subtle daytime smolder to a dramatic evening statement. The key is blending! Start with a neutral primer to ensure your shadows last all day.

Step-by-Step Guide:

  • Apply a dark base color (black, deep brown, or charcoal grey) to your eyelid.
  • Blend a slightly lighter transition shade into your crease, working in circular motions.
  • Add a highlight shade to your brow bone and inner corner of your eye.
  • Line your upper and lower lash lines with a kohl pencil or gel liner, then smudge for a soft effect.
  • Finish with generous coats of mascara.

Experiment with different colors and textures to find your signature smoky eye!

Quick Beauty Hacks for Busy Mornings

Mornings can be a rush, but that doesn't mean you have to sacrifice looking fabulous! Here are a few of my go-to hacks for getting ready quickly.

  • Tinted Moisturizer & Concealer: Skip heavy foundation. Opt for a tinted moisturizer and spot-conceal where needed.
  • Cream Blush & Lip Tint: Use a cream blush on your cheeks and dab a little on your lips for a fresh, cohesive look.
  • Brow Gel: Tame and define your brows in seconds with a tinted brow gel.
  • Curl Lashes & Mascara: A quick curl and a coat of mascara open up your eyes instantly.

These simple steps can make a huge difference when you're short on time!

Exclusively for Deli's VIP members! Finding the perfect foundation is crucial for any makeup look. After extensive testing, I'm thrilled to share my top 5 picks for 2025, ensuring flawless coverage and lasting wear.

1. Flawless Radiance Liquid Foundation

This foundation is a dream come true for anyone seeking a luminous, natural finish. It blends seamlessly into the skin, providing medium-to-full coverage that still allows your natural complexion to shine through. I love how it minimizes the appearance of pores and fine lines, making the skin look incredibly smooth. It's also super long-wearing—perfect for those long days when you need your makeup to stay put without creasing or fading. Ideal for normal to dry skin types.

2. Everlasting Matte Perfection

For my oily and combination skin beauties, this is your holy grail! Everlasting Matte Perfection provides an incredible soft-matte finish that controls shine throughout the day without feeling heavy or cakey. It offers excellent full coverage, blurring imperfections and redness, making it perfect for events or photoshoots. Despite its matte finish, it doesn't settle into fine lines and keeps the skin looking fresh. A true game-changer!

3. Hydrating Glow Serum Foundation

If hydration and a dewy finish are what you're after, look no further. This serum-infused foundation feels incredibly lightweight, almost like a second skin. It provides light-to-medium coverage that evens out your skin tone while giving it a healthy, radiant glow. It's packed with nourishing ingredients that keep your skin moisturized all day, preventing any dry patches. Perfect for those who prefer a "no-makeup" makeup look or have drier skin types.

4. Weightless Buildable Coverage

This versatile foundation is my go-to when I need customizable coverage. You can apply a thin layer for a sheer, everyday look, or build it up for full glam without it ever looking heavy. Its formula is incredibly weightless, feeling comfortable on the skin even after hours of wear. It's suitable for most skin types, offering a beautiful satin finish that's neither too matte nor too dewy.

5. The All-Day Perfection Stick

For quick touch-ups or targeted coverage, this stick foundation is a lifesaver. Its creamy texture glides smoothly onto the skin, providing excellent coverage that can be blended out with a brush, sponge, or even your fingers. It's incredibly convenient for on-the-go applications and offers a natural-looking finish that blurs imperfections. Great for normal to oily skin and those who value speed and efficiency in their routine.
